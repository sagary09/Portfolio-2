About

Yukta ("Yuki") Sagar is a recent graduate of Philadelphia University where she completed her Masters in User Experience and Interaction Design. During her time there she had the opportunity of interning at BizTek People Inc., a recruiting firm in Portland, OR. She found her calling for art and design during her undergraduate studies at Mansfield University of Pennsylvania, after she decided to switch majors from Environmental Science to Graphic Design.

She enjoys road-tripping, the beach, and being on the water.
